Urticaria

dermal

These

wheals

are
sometimes

known

as

‘
hives
’

and

a

an

allergic

reaction.

Hives

rounded

flat-topped

but

are

always

elevated

surrounding

skin.
Allergic

urticaria

medicines

(e.g.

penicillin)

infection,

pollen,

or

fish,

eggs,

citrus

nuts,
strawberries,

tomatoes.)
Physical

caused
