Advance

When

the

‘
Advance

Care

Planning
’

(ACP)

describes

between

an

their

families,

caregivers,

and

healthcare

professionals
about

their

future

wishes

and

priorities

for

care.
The

goal

of

ACP

is

to

clarify

and

document

an

individual
’
s

wishes,

needs,

and

at
the

end

such

their

final

days

are

in

the

place

own

choosing.
