Frailty
Frailty

is

a

state

of

increased

vulnerability

and

functional

caused

declines

Frailty

health

age.

Frailty

may

include

loss

of

muscle

mass

and
strength,

reduced

energy

and

exercise

tolerance,

cognitive

impairment,

and
decreased

reserve,

leading

to

poor

health

outcomes

and

a

reduced
ability

to

recover

from

acute

stress.
Warning

signs

such

falls,

delirium,

immobility,

weight

and

changes

in
chronic

conditions,

mood,

cognition,

and

function

should

The

and

frailty

in

older

is
CGA

that

a

medical

review,

medication

and

discussion

the
older

person

and

their

carers

to

define

the

impact

of

illness

and

create

an
individualised

care

and

support

There

are

many

common

the

Rockwood

Clinical

Frailty

Scale

(freely

available)

-

a
model

of

accumulated

where

more

correlate

with

negative

frailty.
Multifactorial

frailty

includes

medication

adjustments,

exercise
programs

muscle

strengthening

and

resistance

optimisation

of
nutrition,

cognitive

training

(e.g.

and

social

support.
