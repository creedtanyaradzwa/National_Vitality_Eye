Most

Infection

or

The

infection

90%

MTCT

(up

6

30%

ages

6

High

B

infant

B

reducing

MTCT,

and

early

childhood

transmission

of

HBV.

In

universal
HBsAg

of

pregnant

women

alongside

HIV

and

antiviral

HBV
MTCT.
Testing

HBV

MTCT

of
Hepatitis

However,

access

HBV

serology

among

Where

TDF
