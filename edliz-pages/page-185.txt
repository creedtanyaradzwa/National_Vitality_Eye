Prior

assessed

ARVs;

the

Both

before

ART.

Patients

should

be

adequately

counselled

about

appropriate

lifestyle

such

as

safer

(including
consistent,

correct

use

of

condoms),

and

any

other

that

with

adherence

(e.g.,

alcohol,

psychiatric

should

be

addressed.
At

each

clinic

visit

always

screen

for

tuberculosis

using

a

TB

symptom

checklist,
advise

patients

about

adequate

nutrition,

the

importance

of

medicine

and

regular

follow

up

care.

People

taking

ART

should

also

be

regularly

asked
whether

they

are

taking

other

medicines

including

herbal

as

they

interfere

with

the

efficacy

of

ARVs.
Early

treatment

is

and

HIV

improved

HIV

community

Increasing

that

untreated

may

the
development

non-AIDS

cardiovascular
disease,

kidney

liver

Medical

All

(ART)

CD4

i.e.
TREAT

Health

before

ART.
Once

to

HIV

should

adequate

counselling

and

start

ART

on

the

same

or

at

most

a

week.

However,

recipients

of

care

not

ready

to

start

ART,
should

on-going

counselling

and

support

to

assist

them

to

start

ART
.
